Who are we?

Fresh Feast is a restaurant that serves all kinds of food, but is known mostly for its vegetarian and Mediterranean cuisine. It is one of the most sought-after dining locations in the city of Solaris, and it usually operates at capacity. Our focus at The Fresh Feast is to create a variety of foods from scratch using natural and local ingredients. Whether it be Catering, Prepared Foods, or Sandwiches - we've got you covered! Come by or give us a call, we are sure to have a meal solution that will satisfy you and your family!

One smart assistant of Fresh Feast: Betsy

Who is Betsy?

Recently, the restaurant bought the building next-door, and it has expanded its seating area, as a result. With the increase in its capacity, the restaurant is anticipating an equal increase in patrons. However, its current manpower is not enough to handle the uptick in the patrons, especially in short term. So the chatbot called Betsy is designed to handle, primarily, the reservation process. In addition to handling reservation, the chatbot would like to help in promoting the restaurant, at least to the new customers. He is in the lower right corner of the page. Just click it and talk to her if needed.
